Mozilla me enseña

Pagina de Pruebas

En Mozilla, somos una comunidad de:

  • tecnólogos
  • pensadores
  • constructores

trabajando juntos to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web. We believe this act of human collaboration across an open platform is essential to individual growth and our collective future.

Lea el Mozilla Manifesto to learn even more about the values and principles that guide the pursuit of our mission.
